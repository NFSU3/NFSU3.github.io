// About Me

Hi, I'm Ramlal, a passionate cybersecurity student dedicated to understanding and defending the digital world. I study at the National Forensic Sciences University (NFSU) and specialize in ethical hacking, network security, and digital forensics.

I love participating in Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions, hunting vulnerabilities, and building security tools. My goal is to contribute to a safer internet by identifying and responsibly disclosing security weaknesses.

When I'm not hacking (ethically), I'm reading research papers, practising on platforms like HackTheBox and TryHackMe, or exploring the latest threat intelligence reports.
